About Hawkins Shadow Quiz

Hawkins Shadow Quiz is a small fan project inspired by the Stranger Things universe. It is designed as a fun personality-style quiz that matches you with characters based on how you handle fear, loyalty, risk and mystery.

The questions and result descriptions are written in my own words and do not use any official scripts or copyrighted story material. The goal is to capture the spirit of the characters, not to replace the show.

How the matching works

Each answer you choose is linked to one or more characters from the show. At the end of the quiz, the script compares how often you picked each character relative to how often they appeared as an option, and then selects the closest overall match.

This is not a scientific personality test. It is just a light entertainment quiz for fans who enjoy imagining which member of the Hawkins crew they would be.
