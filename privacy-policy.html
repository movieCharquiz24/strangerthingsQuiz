Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how basic information may be collected and used when you visit and interact with the Hawkins Shadow Quiz website.

Non-personal information

When you access this site, standard technical data such as your browser type, device type, approximate region and pages visited may be logged by the hosting platform or any analytics tools that may be enabled in the future. This information is used only to understand how the site is being used and to improve performance and content.

Cookies and third‑party services

This site may use cookies or similar technologies provided by third‑party services such as Google AdSense or analytics providers to:

  • Measure traffic, basic usage patterns and performance.
  • Show relevant ads, if advertising is enabled.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse of the service.

These third parties may use cookies and similar identifiers to serve and personalize ads, measure ad effectiveness and perform other services. Their use of data is governed by their own privacy policies and terms.

Personal information

This quiz does not require you to create an account or submit sensitive personal information in order to play. If you choose to contact the site owner by email, your message and email address will be used only to reply to you and manage communication.

Your choices

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. You may also use browser extensions or built‑in tools to limit tracking or personalized advertising, depending on your region and browser.

Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as the site changes or if new services are added. Any major changes will be reflected by updating the date below.
